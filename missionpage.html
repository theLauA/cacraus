CACRA: MISSION
美國華人權益會

使命
美籍華人權益會的使命是通過提供入籍后的權益咨詢，鼓勵美籍華人投票和選角 ，支持和幫助有意入籍的華人移民來保障華人權益。我們提供非黨派和公正的消息，并 建議投票為他們信任的候選人投票。我們希望通過本機構的服務和活動能夠有小地出盡 及提升華人在美國的素質和地位。我們也希望我們的意見和關注能被市政府，州政府，和聯邦政府聽到。
Mission Statement
Chinese American Citizen Right Alliance's (CACRA) mission is to protect Chinese Americans' rights by educating citizenship rights, encouraging Chinese Americans to vote, providing voter information, and supporting and assisting immigrants who are interested in becoming United States citizens. We suggest individual voters vote for what they believe in and present non-partisan and unbiased information. We hope to improve Chinese Americans' quality development and promote Chinese Americans' status in the United States. We also hope that our suggestions and concerns can be heard and recognized at city level, state level, as well as federal level.
