行使你的權力
華人的投票率在20%-30%左右。低投票率使得華人的聲音和訴求都被忽略。要讓華人的聲音和訴求被重視，要爭取華人權益，提升華人地位，投票就是第一步!

很多人選擇移民到來美國，希望爭取較好的生活，希望能給子女好的教育，而能有好的將來。在美國我們確認人權，宗教自由權，選舉權，發言權，平等機會和社會生活的起碼保障等等。

但確實保障我們的權力亦需要我們自己爭取，而最好的方法是
  1. 成為公民
  2. 登記成為選民
  3. 投票
  4. 參與社區事項
  5. 鼓勵/解釋給其他人知們可以的權力
我們放眼世界，有多少人能有這些權力？我們要爭取我們的權益，要盡量為下一代做準備，為他們舖一條通路，為要將華人的地位更上一層樓。行使你們的公民權利，投票就是第一步。
Exercise your Right
The voter turnout of Chinese Americans is about 20% - 30%. The voice of Chinese Americans would be ignored by this relatively low voter turnout. To have the voice of Chinese Americans heard, to strive for the rights of Chinese Americans, and to promote the status of Chinese Americans, voting is the first step!

Many people immigrate to the U.S., hoping to have a better life and to give their children better education and a bright future. In the U.S., we recognize freedom of religions, human rights, right to vote, freedom of speech, equal opportunities and basic social protection.

However, we still need to strive for our rights in order to truly receive and protect the rights. Below are the best approaches:
  1. Become a citizen
  2. Register to vote
  3. Take action to vote
  4. Participate in local community activities
  5. Encourage others to become citizen and to vote, and explain citizen rights to others
We have to advocate and protect our rights, and fight for the higher status of the next generation of Chinese Americans.
